About Me

Dedicated to the health and well-being of children

Compassionate Pediatric Care

I am Dr. Zahra Ahmad Afia, a board-certified pediatrician with a passion for providing comprehensive healthcare to infants, children, and adolescents. My approach combines medical expertise with a warm, compassionate bedside manner to ensure both children and parents feel comfortable and informed.

With over a decade of experience, I specialize in preventive care, developmental screenings, immunizations, and managing acute and chronic childhood illnesses. I believe in building lasting relationships with families to support the healthy growth and development of every child.

My Services

Comprehensive pediatric care for children of all ages

Newborn Care

Comprehensive care for newborns including wellness exams, feeding guidance, and developmental assessments.

  • Well-baby visits
  • Feeding consultations
  • Developmental screening

Immunizations

Complete vaccination schedules to protect your child from preventable diseases according to CDC guidelines.

  • Routine vaccinations
  • Travel immunizations
  • Flu shots

Well-Child Visits

Regular check-ups to monitor growth, development, and address any health concerns at every stage.

  • Growth monitoring
  • Developmental milestones
  • Nutrition counseling

Allergy & Asthma

  • Allergy testing
  • Asthma management
  • Treatment plans

Developmental Pediatrics

  • Autism screening
  • Learning assessments

  • Emergency coordination
